How to Play:

  • **Goal:** Connect words horizontally across 5 columns to complete the path.
  • **Connect:** Click on a word in the next column that you think logically connects to the currently highlighted purple word.
  • **Correct:** If you choose correctly, a line will form, and the new word will turn green and become the active word (purple).
  • **Incorrect:** A wrong guess costs you a life. The word will turn red and be struck out. You have 5 lives.
  • **Win:** Complete all 5 connections to win! Your time and lives remaining will be displayed.
  • **Daily Puzzle:** There's a new puzzle each day!
